Holiday Information

Customers will create Holidays and this information will reflect at it’s Extensions portal also. Holidays can be Full Day or hours based. Holidays may be used in Time Group features. Customers calls may be affected on the basis of define Holidays. Extensions can’t create holiday, only organization creates holiday list and these holidays are seen in extension holidays list.
