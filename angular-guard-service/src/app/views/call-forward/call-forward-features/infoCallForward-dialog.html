Call Forward Information

Call forwarding is a phone feature that enables users to forward or redirect incoming calls to any alternate number when lines are busy, calls are not answered, or phones are switched off.
Call Forward is used to forward the call as per time, extension status and default behaviour etc. This feature needs to be active by company or organization from there portal to the Extension. Otherwise, it will not be seen at the portal.
Call may be forwarded to Internal extension as well as PSTN number.
